# Omni Recognition

Qwen3-VL delivers powerful omni-recognition capabilities, going far beyond basic object detection. The model can identify animals, plants, people, scenic spots, and a wide variety of objects including cars, merchandise, celebrities, anime characters, and more.

## Capabilities

With upgraded visual recognition powered by broader, higher-quality pretraining, Qwen3-VL can "recognize everything":

- **Wildlife & Nature**: Animals, plants, flora and fauna identification
- **People**: Celebrities, public figures, and person recognition
- **Landmarks**: Tourist spots, monuments, and scenic locations
- **Products**: Merchandise, cars, and commercial items
- **Pop Culture**: Anime characters and entertainment media
- **General Objects**: Diverse everyday items and specialized objects

## Use Cases

- E-commerce product identification and cataloging
- Wildlife and botanical research
- Tourism and location identification
- Brand and logo recognition
- Celebrity and public figure identification
